Data Analyst and Strategy Consultant

Medical Technology Research, Jan 2015 - Feb 2015
London, United Kingdom

As a bilingual freelancer focussing on strategy research and data analysis, I was hired by L.E.K. Consulting to assist them with a short term project. My research focussed on the use of technology during vitreoretinal surgery in the Netherlands.

My work entailed being the key contact person for the Dutch market. I provided translations, conducted interviews with Dutch surgeons and gathered and analysed market data. Through this, I was able to detect market trends and patterns, which I would then present to senior consultants.
