Data Analyst and Strategy Consultant

Medical After Care Research, Nov 2014
London, United Kingdom

As a bilingual freelancer focussing on strategy research and data analysis, I worked on a short term project at Bain and Company. During this project, my research focussed on medical after care.

By gathering and analysing qualative and quantative data I detected and mapped trends in the market of certain medical after care. The key findings of my analysis presented potential market risks and opportunities, which helped to shape the market strategy presented to the client.
