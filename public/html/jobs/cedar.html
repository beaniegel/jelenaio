Student Enterprise Ambassador

Enterprise and Entrepreneurship, Jan 2014 - Jun 2014
Cambridge, United Kingdom

As Anglia Ruskin University's student enterprise ambassador, my goal was to promote enterprise and entrepreneurship to ARU students. To achieve this, I helped organise events and guest lectures and provided feedback to CEDAR related to these events and the student attitude towards them. I was also the organisation's representative for the faculty of Arts, Law and Social Sciences, which meant I promoted various events, such as workshops and business competitions, to the students of this faculty.
